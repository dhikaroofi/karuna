# Couple Photos

Place portrait photos of the bride and groom in this directory:

## Required Files

- `bride.jpg` - Portrait photo of the bride (Dwi Nova Fitriani)
- `groom.jpg` - Portrait photo of the groom (Hasan)

## Specifications

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) or portrait
- **Recommended Size**: 400x400px or higher
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB

## Tips

1. **Centered Face**: Make sure the face is centered in the photo
2. **Good Lighting**: Use well-lit photos
3. **Background**: Simple backgrounds work best
4. **Expression**: Warm, friendly smile

The photos will be displayed as:
- Circular frames with golden borders
- 140x140px on the website
- Centered above each person's name
- Hover effect for engagement

## Editing Tools

- **Online**: Canva, Photopea
- **Desktop**: Photoshop, GIMP
- **Mobile**: Snapseed, Adobe Photoshop Express
